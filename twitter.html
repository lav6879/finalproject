Twitter employs a multi-layered approach to content moderation which include: automated systems and human moderators. Twitter uses algorithms to automatically detect potentially "violating" content. This systems can flag tweets or accounts based on keywords, images, or other criteria. Twitter users can report tweets or accounts for potential policy violations, these reports are reviewed by human moderators who assess the content. Twitter has the power to remove content, issuing warnings to the account owner, or suspend or permanently ban the account.It also provides users with the opportunity to appeal moderation decisions if they believe their content was unfairly removed or their account was wrongly suspended. Twitter, under Elon Musk's leadership, complied with nearly all government requests to remove content, according to Twitter's own data, fully or partially complying with 98.8% of takedown requests since Musk's takeover on October 27.

Unjust Censorship Incidents on Twitter

  • Twitter has been accused of selectively censoring accounts associated with Russian opposition figures or movements. This includes instances where accounts critical of the Russian government have been suspended or restricted.
  • Users from marginalized communities, such as people of color, LGBTQ+ individuals, and religious minorities, have reported experiencing disproportionate censorship on Twitter. This includes instances where tweets discussing discrimination or systemic oppression are flagged or removed, while hate speech targeting these communities remains unchecked.
  • Twitter faced criticism for siding with the Indian government's stance on state censorship during a contentious battle over free speech concerning a documentary that criticized the country's prime minister.
  • Twitter has suspended numerous accounts that monitored the movements of billionaires' private jets, including those of Elon Musk.
  • Musk has reinstated previously banned accounts, such as those belonging to Donald Trump and Andrew Tate, and has also implemented a workforce reduction of approximately 10% at Twitter.
  • Twitter has been caught suspending accounts or restricting tweets that provide valuable information on topics such as reproductive health, contraception, and LGBTQ+ issues.
  • Twitter has been censoring activists and protest movements by selectively removing or restricting content that challenges powerful institutions or governments.
  • Twitter's algorithms sometimes flag historical content or archival material related to sensitive historical events, such as war crimes or civil rights movements, leading to the temporary suspension of accounts or removal of tweets.
