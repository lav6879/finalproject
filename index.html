Do social media platforms limit users' content consumption autonomy?

YES!
Many social media platforms manipulate the content that reaches you by censoring and silencing large movements. Platforms may enforce community guidelines and content policies, which can restrict certain types of content from being shown. So while users have some degree of autonomy, it's not absolute, and their experience is shaped by various external factors.
