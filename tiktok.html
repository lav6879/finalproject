How TikTok is Dangerous

TikTokâ€™s design, immersing users in a continuous flow of snappy content, aims to captivate attention fully. However, this architecture inadvertently fosters an environment where fear is amplified, and misinformation thrives, especially in times of heightened anxiety. Additionally, TikTok's track record of swiftly removing users can empower malicious flaggers to launch targeted attacks, taking advantage of moderation processes that prioritize speed over context. With moderators often facing time constraints and limited compensation, they are pressured to make conservative decisions, leaving little room for nuanced judgment. As a result, online abusers exploit these shortcomings, utilizing flagging tactics to achieve their objectives. TikTok's rapid-paced nature can influence perceptions of unfolding conflicts, as emotive videos may overshadow considerations of legitimacy.

Instances in Which TikTok Manipulated its Content

  • TikTok portrays sex workers as inherently risky and dehumanizes them, perpetuating stigma around their profession.

  • TikTok has discriminated against women, plus size users, and, LGBTQIA+ creators.

  • Content related to certain cultural or religious topics have been censored by TikTok

  • TikTok has censored content discussing human rights issues in China, specifically targeting videos addressing topics such as the Xinjiang internment camps and the persecution of Uyghurs. Accounts sharing such content have been disabled by the platform.

  • TikTok deliberately suppressed videos that used the hashtags #BlackLivesMatter and #GeorgeFloyd
  • TikTok has been accused of suppressing political content that doesn't align with its guidelines or the interests of certain governments, claiming its meant to localize content.

  • Content promoting body positivity, including images of individuals with diverse body types, has been flagged or removed by TikTok's moderation systems

  • TikTok has been accused of censoring content related to sensitive topics such as mental health, suicide prevention, and sexual assault awareness.

  • Tiktok received praise from Russian officials due to its co-operation with them in the removal of "forbidden" content, related to protests in Russia.
